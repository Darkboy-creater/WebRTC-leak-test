About WebRTC Leaks

What is WebRTC?

WebRTC (Web Real-Time Communication) is a technology that enables audio/video calling and peer-to-peer file sharing directly in browsers.

Why do leaks happen?

WebRTC uses STUN servers to discover your public IP. Even with VPN, these requests can bypass the VPN and reveal your real IP.

How to prevent leaks?

  • Use browser extensions like WebRTC Leak Prevent
  • Disable WebRTC in browser settings
  • Use VPN with built-in WebRTC protection
  • Use Firefox which has better WebRTC controls
